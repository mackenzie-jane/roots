About Us

We’re creating a groundbreaking platform that blends the cultural discovery elements of Ancestry.com with a highly interactive, scrollable user interface, designed to help people explore and share their personal heritage in a deeply engaging way. It’s not just about tracing your family tree—it’s about understanding who you are and where you come from, while connecting with others who share your journey.

In an increasingly globalized world, people are seeking to reconnect with their roots and understand their heritage. Whether they’re uncovering lost traditions, learning about ancestral lands, or connecting with distant relatives, this platform gives users the tools to explore their identity in a more meaningful and interactive way than ever before.

How to Add Artifacts

  1. Click on "Profile"
  2. Once in the profile section, press the "Add Artifact" button
  3. Enter all the fields and click "Add" (this will now be visible on your profile)

Press the community button to explore trending posts and hashtags of your communities or press the home button to see artifacts catered to you!
